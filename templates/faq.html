Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Students register using their official UJ email address. Supervisors are selected from a dropdown list during registration.

Ensure you are using your registered UJ email and password. If the issue persists, use the “Forgot Password” option or contact the Ethics Admin.

  • Form A: Research involving human participants (primary data).
  • Form B: Research using secondary data (requires permission, no direct human interaction).
  • Form C: Research using publicly available data (no human interaction).

Each form includes a section for uploading documents. Files must be in PDF format. Ensure the file size does not exceed the system’s limit (e.g., 10MB).

You will receive a system notification once your supervisor has reviewed and either approved or returned your submission for corrections.

After supervisor approval, the Ethics Administrator reviews the application. Depending on risk level, it may be approved immediately (low risk) or forwarded to the REC Committee for review (medium/high risk).

Please email ethics-support@uj.ac.za for technical queries related to the system.
